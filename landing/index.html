Problem?

The Queen Bee problem refers to the critical challenge of identifying the queen bee within a beehive for monitoring and conservation purposes. Queen bees play a central role in hive survival, as they are the only fertile females responsible for laying eggs and maintaining colony structure. Detecting the queen manually is time-consuming and prone to error, especially in large or aggressive colonies. Delayed or failed detection can lead to colony collapse, reduced honey production, and mismanagement. Automating queen bee detection using AI can revolutionize hive monitoring by increasing accuracy, minimizing human intervention, and supporting sustainable beekeeping practices.
